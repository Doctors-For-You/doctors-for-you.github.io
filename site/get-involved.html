Volunteer with DFY

Our volunteer network have always been the key to our emergency response capabilities. At DFY, our doors are always open to welcome young minds who want to give back to their community.
Field Volunteer
DFY welcomes young minds from various backgrounds like doctors, paramedic, health workers, counselors, engineers, WASH specialists, disaster management specialists, administrative management, logistics etc. The field volunteers are expected to participate in various on-ground activities to provide medical relief to the individuals affected with the disaster.
DFY Promoter
As a disaster response team, we constantly look of material support for our humanitarian activities. For this, we need individuals who can promote our mission among the locals and raise materials necessary for our humanitarian cause. Here are the major requirements:
  1. Medicines
  2. Medical equipment
  3. Linen
  4. Sanitary items
  5. Financial support
